    Course Rules

  1.  Use putter only on green.
  2. Call faster players through.
  3. Play max 4 persons per hole
  4. Replce divots.
  5. Replace plug marks on the green.
  6. If a ball has landed on any of the tempory greens in front of or beside a green, the player must drop directly back and not to either side.
  7. Out of bounds: play three of the tea
  8. When straightening a flag a player must drop the pin into the centre of the hole and not pull or push it in his or her favour
  9. While putting, the flag must be left off the putting surface or a 1 stroke penalty will occur to the person who left the flag on the green.
  10. These rules will apply to the Bugler Competition, The Yellow House Competition and all league matches.
  11. 7th & 8th greens free drop without penalty.
  12. All staked trees on the course free drop without penalty.
  13. Trees at the back of the 1st, 12th, 14th & 16th, if a ball is unplayable a 1 stroke penalty will occur and you must drop 2 club lengths not nearer the hole.
